Information

This is the owner of Pizzeria Mamaria speaking, and I’d like to leave a bit of basic info about our place.

Pizzeria Mamaria started as a wild idea back in 1998, around the end of July. I decided to make it real — and that’s why we’re here today, serving the kind of honest, everyday pizza you can rely on.

The original interior of the pizzeria was designed by the well‑known artist Ed Shreeran. He was with us from the very beginning and even performed a small concert here at the start of his career.

You won’t find Ed around these days — sorry folks.

We source our ingredients locally, we care deeply about our customers, and we’re proud of one unusual thing: our pizzeria runs completely without electronics. We simply didn’t want to use them. Here at Pizzeria Mamaria, we believe that human hands can’t be replaced by anything else.


We’re open from 7:00 to 20:00.
